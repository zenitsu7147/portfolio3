jujutsu kaisen

a world of curses and sorcerers


  • what is jujutsu kaisen?


  • It's a supernatural action story created by Gege Akutami. The plot follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes involved in the world of curses after swallowing a cursed object linked to Ryomen Sukuna, a powerful ancient curse. He then joins a school of jujutsu sorcerers who fight supernatural threats.

The franchise includes: The manga Jujutsu Kaisen The anime Jujutsu Kaisen The prequel film Jujutsu Kaisen 0


The cursed world

  • A hidden world exists beneath normal society where cursed energy is born from human fear and negative emotions. Dangerous spirits known as “curses” roam freely, and only jujutsu sorcerers can see and fight them. It is a world where danger exists unseen, shaping everything in the shadows.



Yuji's journey

  • Yuji Itadori starts as an ordinary high school student with extraordinary physical strength. After a life-changing encounter with a cursed object, he becomes part of the jujutsu world. His journey is about protecting others, learning the cost of power, and struggling between humanity and the curses inside him.



Sukuna

  • Ryomen Sukuna, known as the King of Curses, is an ancient and terrifying entity sealed within Yuji’s body. He is cruel, intelligent, and immensely powerful. Sukuna represents pure destruction and constantly threatens to take control whenever Yuji weakens..



Main characters




  • Yuji itadori,A kind-hearted student with superhuman strength who becomes a jujutsu sorcerer after swallowing Sukuna’s finger.

  • Megumi Fushiguro,A calm and serious sorcerer who uses shikigami (summoned spirits) to fight curses.

  • Nobara Kugisaki,A confident and fearless fighter who uses a hammer, nails, and cursed energy in creative ways.

  • Satoru Gojo,The strongest jujutsu sorcerer, known for his limitless power and playful personality.

  • Ryomen Sukuna,The King of Curses, an ancient evil spirit sealed inside Yuji with overwhelming destructive power.

  • Kento Nanami,A disciplined former salaryman who fights curses with precision, logic, and a blunt weapon.
